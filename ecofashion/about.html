  •                              ECOFASHION

EcoFashion is a project inspired by the massive textile industry in India. It's a vital part of our economy, and is estimated to be worth around $160 billion in India alone. Due to the waste this massive industry produces every year, hundreds of landfills are filled with millions of tonnes of textile waste without regard for the environments affected during harvest, production and disposal.
EcoFashion aims to prevent this problem by providing a statistical analysis of the sustainability of textile businesses and consumers' choices, and recommending more sustainable and cost-effective alternatives!
